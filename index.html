Hi! I’m Chris "oGnomo", a composer and sound designer focused on retro-inspired game audio from the 8, 16, and 32-bit eras of gaming. 

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Music Composition, play classical and metal guitar, and have gained hands-on experience in 20+ game jams over the past year experimenting in various tools and styles. I also play D&D and love old CRPGs such as Baldur's Gate and Pillars of Eternity!

I love blending raw chip sounds with a modern twist, using anything from classic trackers to contemporary DAWs, to craft authentic music that fits your game’s identity.
